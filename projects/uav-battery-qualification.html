Problem framing

UAV batteries are safety-critical. Validation must answer hard questions about voltage thresholds, state-of-charge mapping, over-discharge behavior, high-temperature behavior, and safety actions such as Immediate Landing or Return-to-Home. The challenge is to stress the system deeply while protecting production hardware and preserving traceable evidence.

Engineering approach

  • Developed qualification and acceptance test bench setups for UAV battery systems.
  • Created test execution plans, acceptance test procedures, verification reports, and formal compliance records.
  • Designed a dummy-battery setup using modified BMS and relay PCB hardware with resistor-divider circuitry.
  • Executed battery characterization campaigns to map voltage thresholds and SoC behavior.
  • Analyzed results to support reliability assessment and certification activities.

Outcomes and value

  • Enabled repeatable validation workflows for UAV battery acceptance and qualification.
  • Supported destructive or high-risk test scenarios without damaging production batteries.
  • Generated structured documentation suitable for certification and engineering review.
  • Connected bench testing, electrical stress conditions, safety logic, and evidence packages.
